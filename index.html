INTERDICTInterdiction / PiracyMantis · Fighter escort · Boarding · Hauler▼
Interdiction is an income multiplier — you take what others earned. In Pyro the risk/reward is ideal. In Stanton you take crimestat and UEE response. All income figures assume successful intercepts — missed targets or combat losses reduce these significantly. Run with the Interdiction Planner to coordinate roles.
1
Solo light intercept — ~500k–1.5M per successful intercept soloPyro
Ghost Mk II or Sabre approach cold, wait on known JP exit vectors. Snare targets manually (no Mantis needed solo). Works on solo haulers running drugs or minerals. Each successful cargo steal: 1–3M depending on commodity. Use the Hot Routes tab for current commodity prices.
2
PAF (Pyro Arrest Force) bounty farming — ~800k–2M/hr soloPyro
Accept Pyro bounty contracts — PAF targets are NPC haulers and criminals. No crimestat. Best solo income in Pyro without full piracy risk. Combines with Adagio loop — accept PAF bounties alongside salvage contracts and complete both in the same area.
3
Duo JP ambush — Mantis + fighter — ~2–4M/hr combined duoPyro
Mantis sets up 15km off JP exit vector. Fighter holds cloaked 5km from Mantis. Mantis snares first target out of QT, fighter closes and disables. Split: Mantis gets 40% (snare + scanner), fighter gets 60% (combat + boarding risk). Use iPlanner Stanton JP or Pyro JP location. Tier 1 target: Quant haulers (~···/SCU).
4
Full org JP op — 4-6 players — ~4–8M/hr total orgPyro
Full interdiction cell: 1× Mantis, 1× scanner/ECM, 2× assault fighters, 1× boarding ship (Prowler/Cutlass Steel), 1× loot hauler. Mantis snares, fighters disable, boarding team EVAs and steals cargo intact — preserves full commodity value. Loot hauler returns to fence independently. Use Catfight org Discord for session coordination.
5
Drug run escort (counter-interdiction income) — ~1–3M/hr duoPyro/Stanton
Escort drug runners for a cut of the haul. Neon/E'tam/WiDoW runners pay 10–20% of cargo value for armed escort through contested JP lanes. Live prices: Neon ···/SCU · E'tam ···/SCU · WiDoW ···/SCU. Good income for fighter pilots without a Mantis.
6
REP
CFP Pyro enforcement contracts — ~200 XP / ~85k/run CFPPyro
CFP contracts in Pyro are bounty-adjacent — hunt criminal targets for CFP rep. Drops Artimex/ADP armor BPs. Stack with interdiction downtime. No crimestat.
→ NEXT: Duo JP ambush is the first real interdiction income tier. Get a Mantis + fighter pair set up and use the Interdiction Planner for op briefing. Push to org-scale JP ops for the income ceiling. Check the Hot Routes tab for live cargo values before each session.
MULTIMulti-crewRedeemer · Hammerhead · Perseus · MOLE · Reclaimer · Starlancer TAC▼
Multi-crew ships scale income and capability with crew count. Solo these ships are competitive but wasteful — every empty seat is lost DPS, lost income, or lost survivability. Coordinate via org Discord before undocking. Use the Org Ops Planner to assign roles.
1
MOLE — 3-crew mining — ~1.5M/hr per player miningStanton/Pyro
1 pilot + 2 laser operators. Each operator runs an independent mining laser — 3× throughput vs solo Prospector. Split income evenly. Pilot responsibility: navigation, refinery selection, market timing. Operators: coordinate on rock sizes, avoid thermal runaway. Comms required — call rock HP and charge %. Live Quant: ···/SCU
2
Redeemer — 4-crew ERT stack — ~3M/hr per player combatStanton/Pyro
1 pilot + 1 shield/power op + 2 S4 turret gunners. Combined DPS rivals capital-class platforms. ERT contracts pay 400–600k each — at 4+ stacked targets per run this is the highest reliable combat income per player. Roles: pilot calls targets and manages shield triangle, power op adjusts capacitor allocation, gunners call secondary targets. Assign roles before undocking.
3
Starlancer TAC — 4-crew fleet anchor — ~3.5M/hr per player combatPyro
144,000 HP L-shield makes the Starlancer TAC an ERT farming machine. 1 pilot + 1 power/shield op + 2 S4 turret gunners. The L-shield means the pilot can focus 100% on target selection — no evasive manoeuvring required. Stack 8–10 ERT contracts. Pilot flies straight, everyone shoots, the shield absorbs everything. Pledge ship.
4
Hammerhead — 6-crew patrol — ~2.5M/hr per player combatStanton/Pyro
Full crew: 1 pilot + 1 coordinator + 4 turret gunners. 6× S4 turrets firing simultaneously is an overwhelming DPS platform. Roles: pilot handles navigation and shield management, coordinator calls priority targets, gunners assigned sectors (bow/stern/port/starboard/dorsal/ventral). Income scales with target density — best in high-traffic bounty zones.
5
Perseus — 3-crew anti-capital — ~2M/hr per player combatStanton/Pyro
1 pilot + 2 S5 turret operators. Designed for larger targets — Hammerhead-class enemies, capital bounties, station assaults. 2× S5 turrets are devastating against large targets. Less effective vs small fighters — needs fighter escort for close protection. Best used as fleet support, not front-line skirmisher.
6
Reclaimer — 4-crew crew salvage — ~1.8M/hr per player salvageStanton/Pyro
1 coordinator + 2 beam operators + 1 component stripper. Coordinator manages salvage map and target priority. Beam operators: hull stripping in sync, call full canister handoffs. Stripper: EVA to floating components — shields and power plants have the highest sell value. Add an Adagio titanium contract loop alongside for a significant income boost.
→ ORG TIP: Use the Org Ops Planner to pre-assign roles and calculate combined income before the session. All figures above are per-player estimates — actual income varies with target quality, server conditions and coordination efficiency. Redeemer and Starlancer TAC are the highest ceiling per-player at current ERT payouts.

REFUELStarfarer RefuellingStarfarer · Starfarer Gemini · United Wayfarers▼
The highest solo income ceiling in 4.8. The Urgent Fleet Refuel contract in Pyro pays ~1.8M aUEC per run at United Wayfarers Senior Contractor rank. With fast execution this reaches 5M+/hr — beating the Adagio loop and Covalex Master. Requires a Starfarer or Starfarer Gemini and a significant reputation grind to unlock.
1
Buy the Starfarer — ~45M aUEC (Gemini: ~55M) prereqStanton
The Starfarer is one of the most expensive in-game purchasable ships in 4.8. Save using the cargo or salvage tier first. The Gemini variant has S5 turrets vs the standard S3 — better for self-defence in Pyro but not essential for refuelling income.
2
REP
United Wayfarers — grind to Senior Contractor — ~42–46 contracts United WayfarersPyro
United Wayfarers contracts available in Pyro. Run standard Fleet Refuel contracts to build rep — each takes 10–15 minutes. Need approximately 42–46 contracts to unlock Senior rank and the Urgent Fleet Refuel mission. Stack with CFP or PAF bounties between runs to use the time efficiently.
3
Urgent Fleet Refuel — Senior Contractor — ~1.8M per run / ~5M+/hr top tierPyro
The money mission. 1.8M aUEC per successful Urgent Fleet Refuel contract. Run time: ~15–20 minutes including transit. Execution: accept contract → fly to designated fleet location → dock and transfer hydrogen fuel → return to waypoint. Back-to-back with no downtime: ~3 runs/hr = ~5.4M/hr. Higher with faster QT drives.
4
Hydrogen fuel loop — maximising run efficiency — optimise for 5M+/hr advancedPyro
The Starfarer carries hydrogen fuel in its tanks. Keep tanks full before accepting each contract — refuel at Ruin Station or Checkmate between contracts. Shortest route: accept contract → refuel at nearest station → fly to fleet → transfer → collect payment → repeat. Minimise dead transit time. QT drive choice is critical — Atlas L or VK-00 L cuts 2–3 minutes per run.
5
Gemini variant — combat-capable refueller — same income, better survivability optionalPyro
The Starfarer Gemini has S5 turrets vs the standard S3. In Pyro where interdiction is a real threat, the Gemini can defend itself significantly better. The income is identical — the extra cost (~10M more) buys survivability not speed. If running solo in contested Pyro lanes, the Gemini pays for itself in avoided cargo losses.
6
RISK
Pyro interdiction risk management — protect your investment pyroPyro
The Starfarer is 45M+ aUEC. A Mantis snare + two fighters ends a run. Mitigations: (1) fly indirect QT routes to avoid standard intercept lanes, (2) bring a fighter escort for high-value runs, (3) check the Interdiction Planner Hot Routes tab to know which lanes are most targeted, (4) time runs to avoid peak server population windows.
→ INCOME CEILING: Starfarer refuelling at Senior Contractor is the highest consistent solo income in 4.8 at ~5M+/hr. Above this tier only org fleet ops (Hammerhead/Redeemer multi-crew) or lucky high-value interdiction hauls exceed it. The grind to get here (45M ship + 42 rep contracts) is significant — plan it as a long-term goal while running cargo or salvage.

Hauling is the safest income in the game — no buy-in, no loss if something goes wrong beyond your time. Stack multiple Covalex contracts per trip to maximise efficiency. Each contract completion is independent so failed individual legs don't void the stack.
Start with hauling contracts, not commodity speculation
Hauling contracts have zero buy-in — you can't lose money if something goes wrong. Commodity trading requires upfront capital that you can lose to bugs, pirates, or bad route data. Learn the freight system on hauling contracts, then move to commodities once you have 2M+ aUEC buffer.
Level one company hard before spreading rep
Focus on Covalex first. Member rank opens solar routes (+1,000 XP/run). Master unlocks VK-00 and XL-1 QD blueprints — the best quantum drives in the game. Spreading rep evenly across all companies means you stay mediocre at all of them.
Use SC Trade Tools or UEX before every commodity run
No static guide stays current. Prices shift daily, stock drains, and competition changes margins. The best route today is often not the best route tomorrow. Always check live data before committing capital. Bookmarks: sc-trade.tools and uexcorp.space
Base around one planet and its orbital station
Trading works much better when you run a lane: planet TDD → orbital station → back. Lorville + Everus Harbor, Area 18 + Baijini Point, New Babbage + Port Tressler. Improvising every leg burns time and erodes margins. Pick one hub and learn it deeply.
Mid-value commodities beat premium lanes for real income
Gold and Laranite look great in route calculators but low stock, rival traders, and long retrieval cycles often erase the edge. Titanium, Tungsten, Beryl and Copper have worse margins per SCU but you can actually repeat the run. Repeatability beats theoretical maximum almost every time.
Adagio Titanium salvage cargo is the best 4.8 trade income
3M+ aUEC/hr confirmed in 4.8 with an ARGO RAFT. Requires Adagio Senior Contractor rank. The RAFT rents at Lorville for ~67k/day. C2 Hercules scales it much higher. This is a salvage+trade hybrid — you claim wreck cargo (Titanium) and sell it at a commodity terminal. Not a conventional trade route but currently the best aUEC/hr for cargo ships.
Contraband rules
Neon, Altruciatoxin, Maze and other contraband pay well but trigger security scans in Stanton. Eclipse stealth approach or using outlaw stations (Grim Hex, Levski) as intermediary sell points avoids the worst of it. CrimeStat blocks you from Lorville, Area 18 and New Babbage — a blockade that ends your trading career until you clear it.

Open the contract manager → accept all available contracts of your tier before undocking. Each contract tracks kills independently. You don't need to complete them in order — kills count toward all active contracts simultaneously.

No hard cap but the contract board refreshes on a timer. VLRT/LRT: accept 5–8. MRT/HRT: accept 8–12. VHRT/ERT: accept as many as available — typically 5–8 at peak hours. Certs increase payout, not contract count.

CARGO CALL

EXTRACT
"Clear. Extracting. Mantis — hold until we're 5km out."
Never all QT at once. Mantis holds snare as cover until wing is clear.
