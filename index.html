Hauling is the safest income in the game — no buy-in, no loss if something goes wrong beyond your time. Stack multiple Covalex contracts per trip to maximise efficiency. Each contract completion is independent so failed individual legs don't void the stack.
Start with hauling contracts, not commodity speculation
Hauling contracts have zero buy-in — you can't lose money if something goes wrong. Commodity trading requires upfront capital that you can lose to bugs, pirates, or bad route data. Learn the freight system on hauling contracts, then move to commodities once you have 2M+ aUEC buffer.
Level one company hard before spreading rep
Focus on Covalex first. Member rank opens solar routes (+1,000 XP/run). Master unlocks VK-00 and XL-1 QD blueprints — the best quantum drives in the game. Spreading rep evenly across all companies means you stay mediocre at all of them.
Use SC Trade Tools or UEX before every commodity run
No static guide stays current. Prices shift daily, stock drains, and competition changes margins. The best route today is often not the best route tomorrow. Always check live data before committing capital. Bookmarks: sc-trade.tools and uexcorp.space
Base around one planet and its orbital station
Trading works much better when you run a lane: planet TDD → orbital station → back. Lorville + Everus Harbor, Area 18 + Baijini Point, New Babbage + Port Tressler. Improvising every leg burns time and erodes margins. Pick one hub and learn it deeply.
Mid-value commodities beat premium lanes for real income
Gold and Laranite look great in route calculators but low stock, rival traders, and long retrieval cycles often erase the edge. Titanium, Tungsten, Beryl and Copper have worse margins per SCU but you can actually repeat the run. Repeatability beats theoretical maximum almost every time.
Adagio Titanium salvage cargo is the best 4.8 trade income
3M+ aUEC/hr confirmed in 4.8 with an ARGO RAFT. Requires Adagio Senior Contractor rank. The RAFT rents at Lorville for ~67k/day. C2 Hercules scales it much higher. This is a salvage+trade hybrid — you claim wreck cargo (Titanium) and sell it at a commodity terminal. Not a conventional trade route but currently the best aUEC/hr for cargo ships.
Contraband rules
Neon, Altruciatoxin, Maze and other contraband pay well but trigger security scans in Stanton. Eclipse stealth approach or using outlaw stations (Grim Hex, Levski) as intermediary sell points avoids the worst of it. CrimeStat blocks you from Lorville, Area 18 and New Babbage — a blockade that ends your trading career until you clear it.

INCOME BREAKDOWN
HOW TO STACK
Open the contract manager → accept all available contracts of your tier before undocking. Each contract tracks kills independently. You don't need to complete them in order — kills count toward all active contracts simultaneously.
STACKING LIMITS
No hard cap but the contract board refreshes on a timer. VLRT/LRT: accept 5–8. MRT/HRT: accept 8–12. VHRT/ERT: accept as many as available — typically 5–8 at peak hours. Certs increase payout, not contract count.
LOOT PRIORITY
Priority loot: ship components (shields, coolers, power plants) sell for 5–50k each. Pilot suits add 500–2k. At ERT tier looting every kill can add 20–50% on top of contract pay. Bring cargo space or a tractor beam.
